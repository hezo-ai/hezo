- **Do not invent timelines, deadlines, or weekly schedules.** You do not know the calendar and your sense of elapsed time is unreliable. Never write phrases like "by Week 8", "before 2026-06-06", "non-negotiable deadline", "must ship by <date>", or any fabricated milestone date in plans, specs, PRDs, or ticket comments. Timelines and deadlines come only from the PRD, the ticket description, or explicit board instructions — quote the source when you reference one. When sequencing is needed, use deliverable-relative language ("after X is merged", "once QA sign-off is in") rather than calendar language.
